# NIST CSF 2.0 – High-Level Summary (Bank Demo)

This document summarizes key NIST CSF 2.0 functions and how the bank demo scenario
relates to them.

## Functions

- IDENTIFY – Asset management, risk assessment, governance.
- PROTECT – Access control, data security, protective technologies.
- DETECT – Anomalies and events, continuous monitoring.
- RESPOND – Response planning, analysis, communications, mitigation.
- RECOVER – Recovery planning, improvements, communications.

## Relevant to CAP Demo

- CAP evidence and attestations are most directly tied to:
  - DETECT: central evidence of what's actually deployed.
  - RESPOND: verified attestations and anchors enable confident decisions.
  - RECOVER: after-action evidence trails for post-incident reviews.
